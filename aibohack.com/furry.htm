Do-it-Yourself Instructions

Wanna make your own Furry AIBO ?

Follow these instructions:

  • Start with a lower form of life, like a rabbit or Furby or something with interesting hair:

  • Temporarily color it pink. Suggestions include: pink ribbons, fake blood, or a vat of pink food coloring:

  • Deposit pink colored animal in the middle of a pack of wild AIBOs:

  • Skin the animal! Instructional video can be found here .

  • Clean the pelt. Wash off any pink food coloring, blood or other stains. Scrape the pelt clean of any left over guts (it can really smell up your AIBO). Or visit your local arts-and-crafts store and avoid all the gore of the previous steps.

  • Trim the pelt and affix to AIBO. Velcro or strong double sided tape works well for temporary fur. A hot glue gun will make it more permanent.

  • Keep a vacuum cleaner at hand for the excess fur, you never know what it will stick to:

  • Repeat:

    Wait, I'm one of you!
