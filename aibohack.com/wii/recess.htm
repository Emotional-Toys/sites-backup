Global Recession hits Animal Crossing City Folk

These are hard times for the animals in Animal Crossing. Here are some of their tragic stories.

Tom Nook was murdered in a robbery at his store. His orphaned child has taken over the business.

Resetti took a job at a pattern shop. Not selling much these days. The pickaxe may be scaring away customers.

In a more recent photo, because of the lean times, Resetti has lost a little weight.


Border security was underfunded. Originally two old lizards used to patrol the town gate. Now they are only ghosts.


Illegal immigrants and strange holiday-themed creatures are taking over whatever jobs are left.

Recession hits the City too

Hard times in the City as well.

The hair salon was sold.

The most expensive shops aren't safe anymore. Small gangs of stray kittens will rob you for a morsel of food.

Christmas was cancelled. Unemployed reindeer must make ends meet by performing special services in front of boarded up shops.

And we all miss the late Tom Nook and his iron fist over the economy.
