    Regular Item Codes

    Regular items are items you can store in your pockets (inventory), store in your house, drop on the ground or take to other towns.
    Click here for item code list (in English) (or Japanese)
    NOTE: some are 'not used'. Some items have different item codes for the same item. For example there are 8 different codes for 'axe' from a brand new axe to a worn down axe.

    'Terrain' Codes

    'terrain' item codes are for things on the ground (in the world map).
    IMPORTANT NOTE: You can't hold these items in your pockets. Also there are no terrain codes for buildings. These are obvious attempts to fix the 'building seed' problems of AC:WW.

    Terrain code ranges: (more detail to be added later)
    • 0000->0056 Trees (in groups similar to AC:WW)
    • 0057->005A Weeds
    • 005B->0073 Rocks (including special rocks)
    • 0074->0093 Patterns (8 per player, same as AC:WW)
    • 0094 Hole
    • 0095->009D Growing turnips
    • 009E->00BD Flowers (order similar to AC:WW)
    • 00BE->00DD Parched Flowers
    • 00DE->00DF Dandilions
    • 00E0->00E1 Dry clovers (regular, 4leaf)
    • 00E2 up - some weird ones
