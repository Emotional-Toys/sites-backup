Personality Comparison (ERS-2x0 and ERS-31x)

AIBO Personalities

Much of AIBO's personality is a combination of the complex state machine, your interactions, the dog's mood, and the random number generator.
See the table below. This tries to quantify the amount of random and non-random state machine influences in various AiboWare personalities.

DISCLAIMER: The quantified numbers are only a rough indication of the complexity of the personalities, and where that complexity comes from.

At this rough level, you can interpret:
  • # of States = reflects overall complexity of personality (more states usually means more features)
  • "total" number of Conditions = reflects overall complexity too. This is the total number of conditions that will cause state transitions (think of it as the number of "if ??? goto" statements)
  • "random" conditions = the number of that total that are influenced by the random number generator.
  • "train"able conditions = the number of random conditions that you can influnce by training.

    NOTES:

    • "HelloAibo 'B'" and "Evolution Two" are only available in Japan
    • "Spring" is the 2001 Spring AIBO demo personality
    • ROMxxx are the built in demo/diagnostic personalities in their respective ROMs (ie. what you get if AIBO starts up with no stick)

    Generalizations/Conclusions

    • You can see that some of the personality variants are not very bright (eg: Youth A3, Youth B3 on AiboLife 1)
    • Personalities get more complicated with age (Adult more so than Baby)
    • AiboLife 2 personalities are more complex than AiboLife 1 personalities
    • Some personalities are more "entertainment" oriented. For example AiboPal, Explorer, Hello Type 'B' are still very complex, but rely less on random number generation (ie. they have lots of features, but they are triggered by other things)
    • The ERS-7 AiboMind personality uses a different 'learning' and evolution approach. The overall complexity of the state machine still works as a rough-guess metric of the personality complexity.
