Obey Cat 2.51 (for the ERS-210, 220, 31x)

Obey Cat is a demonstration program for features of RCode Plus. Finally updated for RCodePlus 2.51

Supported AIBOs

  • ERS-110/111 not applicable, not supported.
  • ERS-210 supported.
  • ERS-220 supported
  • ERS-311/312/31L supported.

Required Hardware:

  • An ERS-210 model AIBO OR
  • An ERS-220 model AIBO OR
  • An ERS-31x model AIBO (311 Latte, 312 Macaron or 31L Pug)

  • A Pink Programmable Memory Stick (AKA "PMS" or RCode stick) - ERA-MS008 or ERA-MS016, $34->$39
  • A memory stick reader to install the software (Use only Sony brand stick readers)

Installation:

  • Put an available PMS stick into your computer's stick reader
  • Select any folders you see at the root of the stick (usually OPEN-R and CUSTOM) and delete them. Do not format the stick.
  • Download the proper RCodePlus runtime for the model of your AIBO.
    • For the ERS-210: RCode Plus 2.52 'A' (210)
    • For the ERS-220: RCode Plus 2.52 'A' (220)
    • For the ERS-31x: RCode Plus 2.52 'A' (31x)
    • (Save this .ZIP file on your computer. You may want to use it again later.)
  • Unzip this RCodePlus file to the root of your PMS memory stick.
  • Download the ObeyCat Personality: ObeyCat251.zip (.4MB)
  • Unzip that file on top of your RCODE/PMS stick (replacing any files it asks about).

  • Put the memory stick inside your AIBO and start it up.
  • Play with your AIBO. Follow the instructions (see below).

Interacting with Obey Cat AIBO

Obey Cat will generally do little until directed.
Obey Cat will react to different inputs:
  • Wakeup and roar
  • If it falls down, it will get back up (and then complain about his back)
  • Will meow if it remains idle for too long (over 20 seconds)
  • Will purr if you pet/praise it
  • Will yowl if you hit/scold it
  • Will cough up a fur ball if you press his neck
  • Handle a number of voice commands (see below)
  • When walking, it will handle a limited number of voice commands (see below)
  • When walking, it will stop if it runs into something
  • When it hears another AIBO, it will growl like a dog
  • If you press its back, it will let you pick one of six dances
  • If it hears the right kind of music, it will dance

  • Handles ERS-11x Sound Controller commands
    • Stand, Sit, Lie
    • Game Mode move around (front, back, left, right etc)

Obey Cat will react to different voice commands:
  • NEW: "Name Registration" - will record his name
  • NEW: "What's your name" - will speak his name
  • NEW: If it hears his registered name or "AIBO" - he will roar
  • NEW: "Owner Registration" - will record your name
  • NEW: "What's your owner's name" - will speak your name
  • NEW: If he hears your name (the registered owner name) - he will meow
  • NEW: "Let's Play", "Let's Talk" - cheesy mimic mode (his head will flash, talk to him, repeat -- he will stop after 10 tries)
  • "Take A Picture" - will take a picture
  • "Stand up", "Sit down", "Lie Down"
  • "Go Forward", "Go Backward", "Turn Left", "Turn Right" - start walking (see below)
  • "Come Here", "I'm here" (turns to the sound of your voice and walks)
  • "Stop"
  • "Dance" (one of six short dances)
  • "Left leg kick", "Right leg kick" - Kick the ball (lame)
  • Good Boy/Girl (purrs)
  • Good Night (gets ready for bed/charger)
  • When walking, it will handle a limited number of voice commands
    • "Go Forward", "Go Backward", "Turn Left", "Turn Right", "Stop"
    • "Go Forward" - and he will walk normally
    • "Faster" - and he will walk fast
    • "Slow Down" - and he will walk slow
    • "Action one" - and he will walk "Bandy"
    • "Action two" - and he will walk "Small Step"
    • "Action three" - and he will walk "Bandy2"
    • "Action four" - and he will walk "Bandy3"
    • "Come Here, I'm here (turns to the sound of your voice and walks)
    • "Take A Picture" will take an 'Upskirt' photo
    • (ERS-31x only) Use the tail joystick to change the walking direction
Obey Cat can be directed (forward, back, left, right, stop etc) in three different ways. You can use the voice commands or the ERS-11x sound controller (press the Game button first). If you have the AIBO LAN (ERS-210 only), you can use AiboRemote to control your AIBO like a remote control toy.

Note: the dangerous features of Handstands, RollOver and GetUp (off of charger) have been removed for simplicity.

Photos

Photos are stored on the memory stick in the "\PHOTOS" directory as sequentially numbered .JPG files. They are in standard .JPG format (no format conversion required). You are responsible for managing the photos and making sure the stick doesn't get full.

Master Studio

If you have Master Studio, you can adapt these personalities for your own purposes. See the RCode Master Studio Samples for more details.
