[AiboHack] Freeze Evolution Patch for AiboLife 2 and AiboLife 310

Freeze Evolution Patch for AiboLife 2 and AiboLife 310

Overview

If you are tired of the personality of your Adult AIBO, and want to change it to something more active, you can use Brain Surgery. Since the personalty AIBO picks is based on how you treat the dog, after a short while, the "Brain Surgery" personality may be lost as the dog reverts back to the old personality (based on how you treat it).
If you want total control on the evolution and personality of your AIBO, you need to freeze the natural evolution and personality changes.

If you have an AiboLife1 stick, you can install AiboLife 1 Plus on it and use the control panel to disable evolution and personality changes (together or individually)

If you have AiboLife 2 or AiboLife 310 (for the ERS-31x), you can use the following modifications to disable evolution and personality changes (together).

See the Patch Central page for the "Freeze Evolution" patch.
