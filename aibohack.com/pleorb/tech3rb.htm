PleoRB PLEOPM Overview

This section describes some of the things I've discovered by disassembling the PLEOPM default Pleo personality for the PleoRB. It is significantly different than the older Pleos. The motions and sounds are much larger, and the behavior logic as PleoRB grows up is much more complex and takes a lot longer.

PleoRB Evolution (as PleoRB grows up)

NOTE: Updated version (statsRB, ProbeRB and ChangeRB are currently out of date)

Stage age_new property how long to evolve? motion speed basic personality
Birthing 10 2 to 3 active hours (*) n/a idle_birthing
hatchling/vegetative (Stage 1 in manual) 20 3 to 5 active hours (*) n/a idle_vegetative
social development (Stage 2 in manual) 30 4 to 6 active hours (*) 90% idle_social_dev

old age 60 200 years (realtime) 75% idle_old

NOTEs:
  • This information only applies to the PleoRB. The older Pleos have a much simpler evolution.
  • The "how long to evolve" tells you how long before PleoRB to outgrow this stage and moves to the next.

  • (*) For the earlier stages (Newborn, Hatchling, Social Development), only active Pleo time counts. If you leave your Pleo turned off or with the main battery removed, it will not age.
  • (*) For the earlier stages, you can shorten the time needed by touching and playing with your Pleo. For example, to evolve from 'Social Development' to 'Juvenile' normally takes 6 hours Pleo run time. If you leave Pleo alone, it will take the full 6 hours. If you play with your Pleo, it will take less, to as little as 4 hours play time.
  • TIP: for the earlier stages, if you let PleoRB play in your lap for around 5 to 10 minutes, it will take an hour off the evolution time.

  • In the older stages (Juvenile, Adult, Old Age), PleoRB uses its internal real-time-clock, and will age along with real time. Whether Pleo is running or sitting on a shelf with its main battery removed doesn't matter, Pleo is still aging.

  • The time for the Juvenile stage is extremely short. I believe this is a bug. BUT:
  • The older stages (Juvenile, Adult, Old Age) are very similar. The main difference is the motion speed. This may change with future firmware updates.
  • NOTE: when a Mature Adult reaches 1 year of age, it will save itself in preparation for cloning. Perhaps a future feature?

  • Use the statsRB or ProbeRB or ChangeRB to monitor the growth of your PleoRB. NOTE: in current versions reporting on stages Juvenile and later is incorrect. Use the guidelines above instead.

PleoRB DNA

There are several DNA properties that aren't changed during the lifetime of a normal PleoRB. You can examine them with statsRB, ProbeRB or ChangeRB. You can change them with ChangeRB.
  • DNA "Gender" - very little used right now. Hard coded in PLEOPM. There are three settings: Gender Neutral (usual for Green colored PleoRBs), Make (usual for Blue colored PleoRBs) and Female (usual for Pink colored PleoRBs).
  • DNA "Temper" - influences current "Mood". A bad tempered PleoRB will take more care and attention to keep it happy ("Good", "Normal", "Bad")
  • DNA "Courage" - influences current "Emotion".
  • DNA "Physical" - influences current "Health" and current "Physical" fitness ("Strong", "Normal", "Weak")
  • DNA "Activeness" - influences current "Activity" - Not currently used
  • DNA "Intelligence" - influences current "Obedience" and current "Skill" ("Smart", "Normal", "Dumb")

PleoRB Health and Mood

The health and mood report of statsRB/ProbeRB or ChangeRB dump the main properties that describe the current status of PleoRB As you play with PleoRB, these properties will change. These numbers are percentages, in the range of 0% to 99%. Very low values (under 30%) are usually bad for Pleo and it will refuse many commands. But a high value of 99% is not always the best. If you leave your PleoRB turned off for a period of time, some of the mood and health properties will drop to potentially low values the next time you turn it on. They won't go below 9% (ie. don't worry, your PleoRB won't die).
Experimentation is required to find the best fit for you. If you change them with ChangeRB, these changes will be temporary. However as you play with your Pleo, the DNA factors will influence many of the health and mood properties.
The statsRB/ProbeRB/ChangeRB tools will warn you when critical properties are too low.

PleoRB Food


Using the PleoRB RFID food items influences the Health and Mood properties.
  • Conifer or Cycad leaf:
    • Feed goes up 15% - keeps him alive
    • Physical level goes up a lot - keeps him awake
  • Sugar Cane:
    • Mood goes up by 10% (less for bad temper PleoRB, more for good temper PleoRB)
    • Emotion goes up by 5% (less for low courage PleoRB, more for high courage PleoRB)
    • Health goes down by 2% (less for stronger PleoRB, more for weak PleoRB)
    • Feed goes up a bit (2%)
    • Physical level goes up - keeps him awake
  • Chilli Pepper:
    • Physical level goes up a lot, even more than other foods - keeps him wide awake
  • Coffee Leaf:
    • Emotion goes up by 15%
    • Physical level goes up - keeps him awake
  • Mushroom:
    • Feed goes up 5%
    • Health goes up by 5%
    • Physical level goes - keeps him awake
  • Mint Leaf:
    • Cures certain conditions ? ??
  • Rock Salt:
    • Cures certain conditions ? injury ?
  • Chunk Of Ice:
    • Mood goes up by 10%
    • Emotion goes up by 5%
    • Cures certain conditions ? too hot ?
