Animal Crossing Warnings and Teasers

Warning: Building Seeds are dangerous

There is a lot of hype lately about 'building seeds' on AC:WW BBSs (mansion seeds, museum seeds etc). They are dangerous items that can be used to terrorize and break towns over WiFi.
If you have an AR DUO and can use the map editor, you have no reason to use 'seeds' in your town. The map editor does everything possible with 'seeds' much better and safer (you can preview, delete, move, test and not worry about breaking your town). The map editor can do many cool and powerful things that make 'seeds' look very lame in comparison.
The Map Editor now has all of these dangerous 'seeds' in the official release. Seeds are a fact of life. Most people who want to do damage already have them, and have gotten bored of them. Hopefully only a few people will try them, and quickly get bored with them. Play nice.

For more details, see the Danger Level topic

OLDER WARNINGS

(The initial flurry of these problems has died down. The risks are still there. Be careful who you add to your friends list.)
It is possible for evil visitors to screw up your town.

Nintendo released a bogus 'red tulip' which can screw up your game. Get rid of it by giving/selling it to Nook. Nintendo will fix it for you, if you send in your game cartridge and your NDS console. However it is easier to fix it yourself.
This map editor can be used to fix these problems. This map editor is primarily for customizing your own town. The map editor now lets you experiment with similar 'glitched' flowers as well as much more dangerous items.

General Trading Advice:
As always be careful of the visitors you allow into your town. If a complete stranger is offering a giveaway of valuable items, don't let them into your town. Instead go visit their town, and pickup items you see on the ground to take back to your town. Don't take unnamed items from the Lost+Found or Recycler. When you get back to your town, remove the stranger's friend code from your Friends List (so they can't sneak into your town later). That's the safe way to get stuff from strangers.
If you want to be extra safe, get an Action Replay device yourself, backup your town and save it. That way you can always restore a backup of your town to fix any problems.
