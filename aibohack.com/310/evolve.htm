ERS-31x Evolution (with AiboLife)

Original AiboLife 310 (ERF-310AW01) Evolution

ERS-31x AIBOs using the W01 AiboLife software will evolve from stage to stage. The ERS-31x evolution is similar to the ERS-210. There is one less baby stage, and the times between stages have been changed.

NOTES:
  • ERS-311 and ERS-312 will act the same when using AiboLife 310.
  • The experience points, real time and day counts are not reset to zero when a new stage is hit.
  • The names are names I made up based on the ERS-210 Fun Pack. The ERS-31X personalities may or may not reflect these generalities.

    For more details, as well as details on the suggested stage names,     click here for the evolution chart of the ERS-210.
    Be aware that the ERS-210 times are times between stages and the ERS-31X times are times since birth.
    The "experience points" and their thresholds, exist for both ERS-210 and ERS-31X. For the ERS-210 this was explained in terms of "Quality Time" (which wasn't really time). A future version of the ERS-210/310 browser will clean up this information.

    New AiboLife 310 'B' (ERF-310AW10) Evolution

    The new AiboLife for the 31x (currently only available in Japan) supports Bluetooth and the Handy viewer.
    You can use the viewer to adjust the rate at which your AIBO evolves (normal evolution is 162 hours/81 days). With Fast evolution set by the Viewer, it will take one third as long to evolve. With Slow evolution set by the Viewer, it will take three times as long to evolve. You can also freeze evolution with the Viewer.
    The AiboLife evolution chart is somewhat abbreviated (2 baby, 2 child, 1 youth and 1 adult).
