ERS-310 Fake English Language Voice Dictionary

This web page describes all the English voice commands used for Kawaii AIBO and Chatty Life when the proper English Vocab Patch is installed.
If you do no apply the patch, you must speak to your AIBO in Japanese. See this page for tips on phonetic Japanese if you are so inclined.
Most of the standard Aibo Life 310 (ERF-310AW01) voice commands are the same. Commands specific to Aibo Life, like motion training are not supported. New voice commands are somewhat obscure (eg: like the cleaning motion for Kawaii AIBO).
NOTE: dicids may not be accurate
