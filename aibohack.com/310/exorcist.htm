Exorcist AIBO for the ERS-31x


NOT FOR CHILDREN!


You must be 18 years of age or older to use Exorcist AIBO!
Contains very foul language, designed to freak out your adult friends (and maybe a little yourself)
Use at your own risk - can be a little rough on your AIBO
Use only on a soft surface (like a reasonably thick carpet).

Supported AIBOs

  • - This version is for the ERS-311, ERS-312 or ERS-31L (flips on back)
  • - Use the ERS-7 version for the ERS-7 (lies on belly)
  • - Use the ERS-210 version for the ERS-210 or 210A (lies on belly)

Installation

Required Hardware:

  • An ERS-31x model AIBO (311 Latte, 312 Macaron or 31L Pug)
  • A Pink Programmable Memory Stick (AKA "PMS" or RCode stick) - ERA-MS008 or ERA-MS016, $34->$39
  • A memory stick reader to install the software (Use only Sony brand stick readers)

Installation:

  • Put an available PMS stick into your computer's stick reader
  • Select any folders you see at the root of the stick (usually OPEN-R and CUSTOM) and delete them. Do not format the stick.
  • Download the proper RCodePlus runtime for the model of your AIBO.
    • For the ERS-31x: RCode Plus 2.52 'A' (31x)
    • (Save this .ZIP file on your computer. You may want to use it again later.)
  • Unzip this RCodePlus file to the root of your PMS memory stick.

  • Download the Exorcist AIBO Personality: exorcist10.zip (2.5MB)
  • Unzip that file on top of your RCODE/PMS stick (replacing any files it asks about).

  • Put the memory stick inside your AIBO and start it up.
  • Play with your AIBO. Follow the instructions (see below).

Usage

  • Put the stick in your AIBO.
  • Replace the battery cover (for better effect)
  • Place AIBO on a soft surface (like carpet). This is important!
  • The 31x Panda suit is not recommended (AIBO will not flip/roll over reliably)
  • Be sure there is no pink/red in AIBO's line of vision
  • Boot up AIBO

  • Exorcist AIBO will start up in a nice mood making happy baby sounds
  • It will sometimes walk for very short distances
  • Sometimes it will poop on the floor (with gross sound effects)
    If this offends you do not proceed (it gets much worse)

  • Show the AIBO the pink ball
  • The first few times this will say a short audio clip
  • Show him the ball a few more times, and give him room
  • There is no interactivity at this point. Watch the show and wait for AIBO to recover from being possessed.
  • He will flip on his back and flail around. Place a pillow behind his head if you are concerned (especially the first time). Be sure to place him on a soft surface !!!
Everything skit should be unique. The flailing on the ground can get a little boring after a while. If you run the exorcism sequence again after AIBO returns to normal and gets back on its feet (then show him the pink ball 3 times), the skits will get longer.

Another suggestion:

  • Place Exorcist AIBO in a room with an unsuspecting person.
  • Show them the nice robot mood.
  • Give the unsuspecting person the pink ball and suggest they show the ball to AIBO. Conveniently walk out of the room.
