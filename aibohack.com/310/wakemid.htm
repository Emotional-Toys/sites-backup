ERS-31x Wakeup Tunes

Wakeup Sounds

When the ERS-310 starts up, it will play a MIDI tune specific to its current stage. If you listen carefully, you can compare the tune your ERS-310 plays to the list below and determine the current stage of your AIBO.
If you have a memory stick reader, the ERS-310 Browser will give you this information in more detail.
  • Baby A
  • Baby B
  • Baby C
  • Child 1 (either A1 or B1)
  • Child 2 (either A2 or B2)
  • Youth 1 (either A1 or B1)
  • Youth 2 (either A2 or B2)
  • Adult (either 1, 2, 3 or 4)

Growing (Evolution) Sounds

When the ERS-310 evolves to a new stage, it will play a different tune.
  • Grow to Baby B
  • Grow to Baby C
  • Grow to Child A1
  • Grow to Child A2
  • Grow to Child B1
  • Grow to Child B2
  • Grow to Youth A1
  • Grow to Youth A2
  • Grow to Youth A3
  • Grow to Youth B1
  • Grow to Youth B2
  • Grow to Youth B3
  • Grow to Adult 1
  • Grow to Adult 2
  • Grow to Adult 3
  • Grow to Adult 4

AiboPal Wakeup Sounds

For AiboPal, there are four different personality variants. If you listen carefully, you can compare the tune your ERS-310 plays to the list below and determine the current stage of your AIBO.
  • Latte Positive (good temper)
  • Latte Negative (ill temper)
  • Macaron Positive (good temper)
  • Macaron Negative (ill temper)
