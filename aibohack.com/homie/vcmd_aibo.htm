HomieGate - What to Say or Ask (AIBO Specific)

Aibo Tricks

Try the following with your AIBO:
  • "Are you hungry", "Are you tired", "Check battery level"
  • "Are you hot", "Check temperature"

  • "look left", "the other right"
  • "look right", "the other left"
  • "look up", "look at me"
  • "look down"

  • "Sit down", "Sit up"
  • "Lie down"
  • "Stand up"
    NOTE: most of the time AIBO should be sitting
    NOTE: some of the skits will start from a lie down posture. This makes them a little "jumpy".

  • "Hello how are you?", "Bonjour come on sa va?"
  • "what do you know?"
  • "Hello AIBO"
  • "how old are you"
  • "what is your name?"
  • "who is your owner?" (NOTE: you can customize this in 'gsrc\aibo.g')
  • "what is your language?", "what is your native tongue?"
  • "what is your version?"
  • "when is/was your birthday?"
  • "who are your friends?"
  • "what are you wearing?"

Skits / Impressions

AIBO can do many impressions:
  • "do you want a scooby snack?"
  • "sing the under pants song"
  • "sing the cat food song"
  • "sing the tiger song"

  • "perform the bender attention skit"
  • "perform the bender dream skit"
  • "perform the bender drunk skit"
  • "perform the bender party skit"
  • "perform the bender religion skit"
  • "perform the bender religion speech"
  • "perform the bender other speech"
  • "perform the bender monkey speech"
    NOTE: you can say "do the" instead of "perform the" in the previous skits

  • "act like bender"
  • "act like scooby doo"
  • "act like astro"
  • "act like eric cartman"
  • "act like a human baby"
  • "act like homer simpson"
  • "act like a mad cow"
  • "act like pikachu"
  • "act like kittens"
  • "act like a cat"
  • "act like a tiger"
  • "act like elvis"
  • "act like richard nixon"
    NOTE: you can say "impersonate" instead of "act like" in the previous skits

NOTE: HomieGate may have problems playing back longer skits/dances.
NOTE: some of the skits will start from a lie down posture. This makes them a little jumpy/jerky. Keep AIBO on a soft surface. If you don't like your AIBO jerking around that much, don't make him perform the skits.
