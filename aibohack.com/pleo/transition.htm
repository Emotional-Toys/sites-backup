Background Story

Recently "Innvo Labs" bought the intellectual property from bankrupt UGOBE. This includes the Pleo software, PDK and other things. My software starting in 2007 was using an "NDA" version of the PDK (owned by UGOBE) and an agreement with UGOBE to let me distribute it.

More recently (Sept/Oct 2009) Innvo Labs released a PDK. Unfortunately their terms are incompatible with my software, especially the restrictions on reverse engineering. Considering the main Pleo software is not open source, IMHO a fair bit of reverse engineering is required to anything non trivial.

My Pleo software may disappear at a moments notice. Please ask the new owners "Innvo Labs" to move to an open source model. See comments here (for now until someone points me to a better forum)
