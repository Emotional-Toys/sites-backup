Built in Sounds

There are 30 sounds built into ICybie. The sounds are the same for all known versions of the ROM.

  • 01 SOUND_BEEP - tone/beep
  • 02 SOUND_YIP - tone/yip
  • 03 SOUND_YUP - tone/yup
  • 04 SOUND_BARK - standard bark
  • 05 SOUND_BARK2 - another bark
  • 06 SOUND_BARK3 - another bark
  • 07 SOUND_BURP - burp
  • 08 SOUND_LICK - lick four times
  • 09 SOUND_IN_PAIN - whine (if low battery)
  • 10 SOUND_PANT - pant
  • 11 SOUND_DRINK - drinking water
  • 12 SOUND_GROWL - growl
  • 13 SOUND_YOU_ASS_HOLE - talking
  • 14 SOUND_HOWL - howl
  • 15 SOUND_RURUFF - talking
  • 16 SOUND_PANT2 - more panting
  • 17 SOUND_PANT3 - more panting
  • 18 SOUND_WATER_SFX - peeing finale
  • 19 SOUND_RURUFF2 - talking
  • 20 SOUND_MELODY - greeting musical tune
  • 21 SOUND_LICK_ONCE - lick
  • 22 SOUND_SNEEZE - sneeze
  • 23 SOUND_SNIFF - sniffing
  • 24 SOUND_SNORE - snoring
  • 25 SOUND_PEE_SFX - peeing (the largest sound)
  • 26 SOUND_WHINE - whining
  • 27 SOUND_WHINE2 - more whining
  • 28 SOUND_RURUFF3 - talking
  • 29 SOUND_YAWN_RUFF - yawn
  • 30 SOUND_SCREECH - tone/screech

    Technical Info

    Sounds are stored in a 4bit SunPlus proprietary ADPCM format. The sample rate is 8KHz (ie. 4000 bytes per second). The data is sent from the main ROM (or cartridge ROM) to the sound daughter board where it is converted to analog.
