[AiboHack] Exorcist AIBO for the ERS-7

Exorcist AIBO for the ERS-7


NOT FOR CHILDREN!


You must be 18 years of age or older to use Exorcist AIBO!
Contains very foul language, designed to freak out your adult friends (and maybe a little yourself)
Use at your own risk - can be a little rough on your AIBO
Use only on a soft surface (like a reasonably thick carpet).

Supported AIBOs

  • - This version is for the ERS-7 (lies on belly)
  • - Use the ERS-210 version for the ERS-210 or 210A (lies on belly)
  • - Use the 31x version for the ERS-311, ERS-312 or ERS-31L (flips on back)

Installation (install both zip files in order)

  • Dig up an RCODE/PMS memory stick (8MB or 16MB). It must be a PMS stick. It must be pink. It must not have an ERF- number on the sticker. You can't use the 32MB AiboMind pink stick.

  • Delete any files on the stick (do not format, use a Sony brand stick reader)
  • Download the RCODE7+YART memory stick image: RCODE7_YART703_E.zip (3.8MB)
  • Unzip the memory stick image on top of your RCODE/PMS stick.

  • Download this file: exorcist7.zip (2.2MB)
  • Unzip it to the root of the memory stick. It will ask you to replace some files. Answer "Yes".

  • Put the memory stick inside your AIBO and start it up.
  • Play with your AIBO. Follow the instructions (see below).

    Usage

    • Put the stick in your AIBO.
    • Place AIBO on a soft surface (like carpet). This is important!
    • Be sure there is no pink/red in AIBO's line of vision
    • Boot up AIBO

    • Exorcist AIBO will start up in a nice mood making happy baby sounds
    • It will sometimes walk for very short distances
    • Sometimes it will poop on the floor (with gross sound effects)
      If this offends you do not proceed (it gets much worse)

    • Show the AIBO the pink ball
    • The first few times this will say a short audio clip
    • Show him the ball a few more times, and give him room
    • There is no interactivity at this point. Watch the show and wait for AIBO to recover from being possessed.
    • He will lie on the ground flail around. Be sure to place him on a soft surface !!!
    If you run the exorcism sequence again after AIBO returns to normal and gets back on its feet (then show him the pink ball 3 times), the skits will get longer.

    Another suggestion:

    • Place Exorcist AIBO in a room with an unsuspecting person.
    • Show them the nice robot mood.
    • Give the unsuspecting person the pink ball and suggest they show the ball to AIBO. Conveniently walk out of the room.
