[AiboHack] ERS-7 AiboMind "Touch of Japanese" voices

ERS-7 AiboMind "Touch of Japanese" voices

Getting tired of AIBO all of a sudden talking to you in English ? After you get some experience with AIBO's modes, this can be annoying.

Based on an idea and testing from Virtuahead, comes "a touch of Japanese".
There are two custom sound packages that will replace some of the more obvious English voice prompts and sayings with the equivalent Japanese ones. For those people who prefer their dog to talk Japanese (instead of spontaneous English). You won't understand what the dog is saying, which is the entire reason for doing this.

All voice commands remain in English, the AiboMind software remains officially in English (ie. you can add other English custom data) and most of the more detailed voice prompts are in English (ie. when you must follow the instructions).

If you actually understand Japanese, you should be running the Japanese AiboWare (everything is said in Japanese, and all voice commands are in Japanese)

Comes in two flavor voices, "Echo Male" (the normal Japanese AiboMind voice of a male voice with a lot of echo) and Girl1 (one of the free Japanese AiboMind voices). You can install both. Install Echo Male" first, write it to the memory stick, then install "Girl1".

Instructions

  • Backup your AiboMind memory stick first.

  • Download this self extracting ACD data file apacd_touchofj.exe (1.9MB)
  • Run it on your PC. If you have installed the ACM in the recommended folder on your C: drive, you can use the default unzip location.
  • After installation you don't need the apacd_touchofj.exe file/program anymore (it has installed the necessary ACM data onto your PC).

  • Run the AIBO Custom Manager (ACM) software. You should now see two new entries in the "Custom Data List" called "Touch of Japan - Echo Male" and "Touch of Japan - Girl1"
  • Insert your AIBOMind memory stick into your PC memory stick reader.
  • Inside the ACM program:
    • Click the "Drive Path" to point to your memory stick drive.
    • You will see any existing customizations in the list on the left hand side.
    • Double click on one of the "Touch of Japan" custom data (in the right hand list)
    • It should now appear in the left hand side "Custom Data List"
    • Press the "Write" button (bottom of the ACM) and click "OK" when asked
    • Wait while the memory stick is being updated
    • When complete, you normally don't need to eject the stick (on Windows XP)
    • Exit the ACM

  • Put the memory stick in your AIBO and start him up
  • To go back to the original male voice, just copy the AUDIO.ODA from your backup, or restore the entire backup.

NOTE: based on voice data that is freely available to Japanese ERS-7 owners. There are some additional ERS-7 voice prompts (not included here). They are not free and require a Japanese credit card and a full Japanese AiboMind stick.
