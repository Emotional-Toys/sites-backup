What the heck is "BrainBo"?

BrainBo is a system of several components that makes your AIBO very intelligent. BrainBo can understand thousands of words and phrases and can reply in English. Ask BrainBo "Why is the sky blue?" and she will respond "The sky is blue because of the atmospheric optical effect known as Raleigh scattering."

BrainBo is the best name I could come up with.

When BrainBo is running, it will send the audio it hears to your PC. The PC will perform voice recognition on the audio. The PC will use a set of "grammar" rules to decide what you have said, and how it should react. The PC will synthesize the audio and motions as a reaction of what it hears. Finally, the PC will transmit the audio and motions to the AIBO to perform them.

Eliza and Alice

BrainBo is sort-of like Eliza or Alice. These programs allow you to type in arbitrary phrases and perform simple keyword and phrase detection to decide how to respond. Some of the knowledge in the BrainBo database is derived from the Alice Bot "AIML" files.

BrainBo is female, following the example of Eliza and Alice. This is also because the Text-To-Speech generated voice sounds better at a higher pitch (even though AIBO playback is limited to 8kHz). You can change the gender of BrainBo by saying "talk like a man".

Required Hardware:

  • An ERS-210 model AIBO OR
  • An ERS-220 model AIBO

  • A Pink Programmable Memory Stick (AKA "PMS" or RCode stick) - ERA-MS008 or ERA-MS016, $34->$39
  • A memory stick reader to install the software (Use only Sony brand stick readers)
  • An AIBO LAN Card

  • A relatively fast PC (500 minimum, 1GHz recommended)
  • 128MB of RAM is recommended
  • A PC LAN Card

BrainBo requires a PC LAN connection, and a rather fast PC computer (which performs the voice recognition and other processing). 500MHz or faster is recommended (the faster the better).

If you have run "AiboScope LIVE!" or "AiboRemote" or the Sony AIBO Navigator, you are most of the way there.

Required Software:

  • BrainBo software (RCode Plus 1.90) downloaded to the PMS memory stick
  • Proper network configuration of the PMS stick
  • Windows XP OR installation of "Speech SDK 5.1" support files (68MB download!)
  • The BrainBo "Talk3" program (and support files) on the PC
