ERS-210 Color Preferences

New Colors

The ERS-210 has different color awareness than the ERS-11x. The BALL color is pink and red. The FAV color is blue. The UNFAV (hated color) is a very hard to detect yellow-green. There is a new color for Flesh. Here are the colors for the ROM version of AiboWare:
