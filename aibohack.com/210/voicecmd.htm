ERS-210 Voice Commands

Voice Commands - W01,W02

Here are the English voice commands for the ERS-210 W01 (AiboLife) and W02 (Hello AIBO).
For other languages or more detailed information about pronunciation, phrase variations or other voice command vocabularies, check these pages:
  • English
  • Japanese
  • French.
  • German.

Note: each voice command can have one or many different phrases.
The voice recognition works best on longer phrases.

Number English Description
Vcmd# 1 "AIBO" the dog responds (or registered name)
Vcmd# 2 "Shake Hand" Shake a paw
Vcmd# 3 "Let's Play", "Let's Talk" becomes playful, including Mimic mode
Vcmd# 4 "How old are you" displays the dog's stage (see manual)
Vcmd# 5 "Go Back" dog takes a step back
Vcmd# 6 "Other Hand", "Other Paw" shake left paw
Vcmd# 7 "Get Up" Waken up by hearing its name
Vcmd# 8 "Sit down" dog sits
Vcmd# 9 "Good Boy", "Good Girl" praise
Vcmd#10 "Shake" picks up several short words
Vcmd#11 "Are you hungry" displays something else
Vcmd#12 "Good Morning" greeting, makes the dog more gentile
Vcmd#13 "Good Night" greeting
Vcmd#14 "Karate Chop" does a karate chop
Vcmd#15 "Go for it" some form of encouragement
Vcmd#16 "Kick the Ball" find and kick the ball
Vcmd#17 "Are you alright" makes the dog know you care
Vcmd#18 "Say Hello" Aibo will greet you / shake right paw
Vcmd#19 "Hello" greeting
Vcmd#20 - (not used)
Vcmd#21 "Be Quiet" ends Mimic mode
Vcmd#22 "Take a Picture" enter photo mode (see manual)
Vcmd#23 - (not used)
Vcmd#24 "Stop" dog stops moving
Vcmd#25 "I like you [so much]", "I love you [so much]" get your emotional needs met by a plastic robot
Vcmd#26 "I am here" matches "Come Here"
Vcmd#27 "Stand up" dog stands
Vcmd#28 "Don't do that", "Don't do it" scold
Vcmd#29 "Let's Dance" start a little dance
Vcmd#30 "Are you tired" displays something
Vcmd#31 "Name Registration" enter name registration mode (see manual)
Vcmd#32 "What's your name" the dog says its name
Vcmd#33 "Goodbye", "Bye Bye" greeting
Vcmd#34 "Turn Left", "Go Left" dog turns
Vcmd#35 - (not used)
Vcmd#36 "Lay Down" dog lays down
Vcmd#37 "Pose", "Pose for me", "Take a pose" strike a pose (AKA Vogue)
Vcmd#38 "Find the ball","Where's the ball" tries to find the ball
Vcmd#39 "Go Forward" dog walks
Vcmd#40 "Turn Right", "Go Right" dog turns
Vcmd#41 n/a joyful motion (Japanese only)
Vcmd#42 n/a angermotion (Japanese only)
Vcmd#43 n/a special motion (Japanese only)
Vcmd#44 n/a secret motion (Japanese only)
Vcmd#45 "Let's Go" gets dog into color tracking mode
Vcmd#46 "How Cute" limited use (Child B2 only)
Vcmd#47 "Bad Boy", "Bad Girl" limited use (Child B2 only)
Vcmd#48 - (not used)
Vcmd#49 "Roar" matches "Rollover" (not available in French)
Vcmd#50 "Meow" or other short words like "Moo" (not available in French)
Vcmd#51 - (not used)
Vcmd#52 "Walk Around" causes dog to go wandering
