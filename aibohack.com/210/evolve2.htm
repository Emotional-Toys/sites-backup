ERS-2x0 Evolution "AiboLife 2"

An ERS-210 or 220 AIBO using the new AiboLife 2 memory stick will evolve from stage to stage. The evolution path is very similar to the ERS-31x AiboLife ("AiboLife 310") with slightly shorter evolution times. The evolution times are significantly longer than in AiboLife 1, and in general the personality is different.
