ERS-210 Wakeup Tunes

Wakeup Sounds

When the ERS-210 starts up, it will play a MIDI tune specific to its current stage. If you listen carefully, you can compare the tune your ERS-210 plays to the list below and determine the current stage of your AIBO.
If you have a memory stick reader, the ERS-210 Browser will give you this information a lot easier.
  • Baby 1/Baby A
  • Baby 2/Baby B
  • Baby 3/Baby C
  • Baby 4/Baby D
  • Child A1
  • Child A2
  • Child B1
  • Child B2
  • Youth A1
  • Youth A2
  • Youth A3
  • Youth B1
  • Youth B2
  • Youth B3
  • Adult 1
  • Adult 2
  • Adult 3
  • Adult 4

Growing (Evolution) Sounds

When the ERS-210 evolves to a new stage, it will play a different tune.
  • Grow to Baby 1
  • Grow to Baby 2
  • Grow to Baby 3
  • Grow to Baby 4
  • Grow to Child A1
  • Grow to Child A2
  • Grow to Child B1
  • Grow to Child B2
  • Grow to Youth A1
  • Grow to Youth A2
  • Grow to Youth A3
  • Grow to Youth B1
  • Grow to Youth B2
  • Grow to Youth B3
  • Grow to Adult 1
  • Grow to Adult 2
  • Grow to Adult 3
