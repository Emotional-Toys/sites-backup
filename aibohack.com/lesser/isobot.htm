i-Sobot

i-Sobot and other robots

i-Sobot is tiny!

NOTE: for size comparison, pencil is 7.5". The i-Sobot has limited grip capabilities (it can't hold a pencil on its own)

i-Sobot technical details

The remote uses a 39kHz/40kHz modulated IR signal. Details of the format can be figured out, if anyone cares.

Inside the i-Sobot are 4 epoxy blobs for chips. Zero technical information is known.
The two main blobs are on a daughter board. There is a 5 pin header for possible expansion (power, ground and a few unknown signals).
There are two blobs on the back. This board connects to the different servos using a 4 pin header (4 of them). There is also a 6 pin header.
There is one daughter board on the back with a single chip (LMV358 dual output amp, ROHS compliant). Unknown why they didn't put it on the main board (which has similar surface mount parts).
Someone may be able to reverse engineer the chips and interface. It would be much easier if they released some technical specs.
